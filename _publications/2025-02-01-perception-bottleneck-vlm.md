**Authors**: Junteng Liu, Weihao Zeng, Xiwen Zhang, Yijun Wang, Zifei Shan, Junxian He

**Venue**: Arxiv, 2025

**Code**: [Vision4Chart](https://github.com/Vicent0205/Vision4Chart)

This paper investigates the perception bottleneck in vision-language models (VLMs) when understanding charts. Through systematic analysis, the authors identify key limitations in how VLMs process visual chart information and propose targeted improvements to enhance chart understanding capabilities.
