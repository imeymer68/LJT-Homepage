**Authors**: Shiqi Chen, Miao Xiong, Junteng Liu, Zhengxuan Wu, Teng Xiao, Siyang Gao, Junxian He

**Venue**: ICML 2024

This paper proposes a novel approach to hallucination mitigation by leveraging the in-context sharpness of inner representations in large language models. The authors demonstrate that sharpness patterns can serve as effective alerts for detecting hallucinations, enabling improved mitigation strategies from an inner representation perspective.
