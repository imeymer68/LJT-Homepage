**Authors**: Junteng Liu, Yuanxiang Fan, Zhuo Jiang, Han Ding, Yongyi Hu, Chi Zhang, Yiqi Shi, Shitong Weng, Aili Chen, Shiqi Chen, Yunan Huang, Mozhi Zhang, Pengyu Zhao, Junjie Yan, Junxian He

**Venue**: Arxiv, 2025

This paper introduces SynLogic, a framework for synthesizing verifiable reasoning data at scale to improve logical reasoning capabilities in large language models. The approach generates diverse reasoning tasks with verifiable ground truth, enabling scalable training and evaluation of reasoning abilities.
