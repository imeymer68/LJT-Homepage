**Authors**: Jinghan Zhang, Shiqi Chen, Junteng Liu, Junxian He

**Venue**: NeurIPS 2023

This paper explores the composition of parameter-efficient modules using arithmetic operations. The approach enables flexible and efficient model adaptation by combining different modules through addition, subtraction, and other operations, offering new ways to customize pre-trained models for diverse tasks.
